The Offering

A New Standard in Sedona Living

Tucked into the exclusive Copper Canyon Homes enclave in Foothills South, 20 Calle Del Bella is a brand new mid-century modern residence crafted by TS Construction — a builder known for meticulous attention to detail and high-end finishes that elevate every surface, every sightline, every moment of daily life.

This three-bedroom, three-and-a-half-bathroom home sits on nearly half an acre and offers panoramic red rock views, expansive walls of glass, and an open floor plan that seamlessly connects indoor and outdoor living. Slated for completion in Q2 2026, this is an opportunity to own one of the first residences in a thoughtfully planned nine-home community — private, serene, and minutes from everything Sedona offers.

"There are properties . . . and then there are opportunities to create something truly lasting."

The Residence

Modern Design, Timeless Craft

Every detail of this home has been considered. The open floor plan seamlessly blends living, dining, and a high-end kitchen designed for both everyday comfort and effortless entertaining. Natural light floods the space through expansive windows that frame Sedona's iconic red rock formations, city lights, and mountain panoramas.

The primary suite features floor-to-ceiling glass, a full en-suite bathroom with double vanity, and direct access to the outdoor living area. A three-car garage, single-story layout, and a metal roof ensure both convenience and longevity.

  • High-end kitchen with premium appliances

Copper Canyon Homes

A Thoughtfully Designed Nine-Home Community

Copper Canyon Homes is a custom luxury micro-community created for buyers who value privacy, design freedom, and a deeper connection to Sedona's landscape. Limited to only nine residences, the community balances exclusivity with a peaceful setting — no through traffic, no crowding, no sense of being on display.

The vision is already taking shape: one home has been completed and sold, one is currently under construction, and seven additional homesites remain available for those seeking to build something entirely their own.
